Who We Are

The Cambridge Reviewer is a place where you can look for Cambridge attractions,
read reviews by fellow visitors and leave your own review.

Created by an all-female team of web developer students, it gives you a space to voice your opinion
and share it with fellow Cambridge residents and visitors.

Get exploring the city and tell us - and everyone else - what you thought of it!
